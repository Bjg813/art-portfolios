About me

I have been passionate about filmmaking most of my life. I fell in love with this art form when I held my first camera at the age of seven. My passion grew when I started studying Film at California State University, Dominguez Hills. Now that I have won a few major awards, my passion has been bolstered, and I know I can keep working in this industry tirelessly.

What makes me stand out from the rest is that my knowledge is very wide, and I already know what it takes to be successful in all stages of production in order to meat a deadline. Through my internship I’ve had the opportunity to work with cameras in multiple camera productions, write scripts, direct, and stage manage.

Through my internship I’ve also had the opportunity to build connections in the industry and work for multiple award winning producers in both production and post production as a freelancer.
