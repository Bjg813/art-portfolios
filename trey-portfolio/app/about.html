About me

Born and raised in Sweden I came to America as a 19 year old with the intention of becoming a professional soccer player. Inspired by the film and tv culture in Los Angeles I decided to change direction and pursue another dream of mine which was creating documentaries. As I’ve brought my visions through pre production, production, and post production to become finished products.

What makes me stand out from the rest is that my knowledge is very wide, and I already know what it takes to be successful in all stages of production in order to meat a deadline. Through my internship I’ve had the opportunity to work with cameras in multiple camera productions, write scripts, direct, and stage manage. Through my internship I’ve also had the opportunity to build connections in the industry and work for multiple award winning producers in both production and post production as a freelancer.
